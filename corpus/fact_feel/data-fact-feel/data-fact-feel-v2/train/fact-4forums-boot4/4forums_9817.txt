Common Sense laws are:  1) Instant background checks with no waiting period.  2) No central database of gun owners.  3) No gun buy backs.  4) No gun confiscations.  5) No gun ownership for felons.  6) No gun ownership for the insane.  7) No gun ownership for family violence offenders.  8) Increased budget for law enforcement of firearm laws.  9) No increased budget for law enforcement and gun control on same bill before Congress.
