Except that in some things, Ham and others cannot so judge. Their axioms are, by their lights, not congruent with reality but an accurate description of it. Note two standard responses where there is an apparent incongruity: we've misinterpreted what the bible says*; or, this will all be explained at the end of days.
In all other contexts, we'd describe such people as insane, of course. Here we merely deem them pious.
* Note that, for the literalists, "firmament" became "expanse" once we knew that the heavens were not a solid object into which stars could be affixed.
